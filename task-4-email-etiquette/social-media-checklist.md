# Social Media Checklist for College Students

## Do's
- Think before you post.
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone.
- Protect your personal information and privacy.
- Verify information before sharing.
- Use social media for learning and networking.

## Don'ts
- Do not share offensive or harmful content.
- Avoid oversharing personal details.
- Do not engage in online arguments or trolling.
- Avoid posting during emotional moments.
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation.
